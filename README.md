# Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI Project

## Overview

The Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional business trends. The project transforms raw retail sales data into interactive and insightful dashboards that support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard provides a complete view of business operations through Sales Analytics, Profit Analytics, Customer Analytics, Product Analytics, and Regional Performance Analysis. Advanced Power BI features such as KPI Cards, Drill-Down, Drill-Through, Interactive Maps, Dynamic Titles, Tooltips, Conditional Formatting, and Slicers were implemented to enhance user experience and analytical capabilities.

## Key Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing products, customers, and regions.
* Evaluate customer purchasing behavior and segment contribution.
* Monitor sales and profit trends over time.
* Assess the impact of discounts on profitability.
* Support strategic business decisions through interactive reporting.

## Key Features
